All rent payments processed through RENTIVA.ai are secured via Razorpay Payment Gateway. We do not store your raw credit card or UPI PIN data on our servers.

By initiating a payment, you agree to Razorpay's terms of service. Tenants are responsible for ensuring sufficient funds before transaction initiation. Standard payment gateway processing fees may apply as dictated by the payment provider.

Return & Refund Policy

Subscription Refunds: RENTIVA.ai subscription fees (Pro plans) are non-refundable once the billing cycle has started. You may cancel your subscription at any time to prevent future billing.

Rent Payment Refunds: RENTIVA.ai acts solely as a facilitator for rent transfers. Any disputes regarding rent overpayment, returns, or refunds for the actual rental property must be resolved directly between the Tenant and the Property Owner. If a technical error occurs during routing via Razorpay, it will be automatically refunded to the source account within 5-7 business days.

Privacy Policy

We take your privacy seriously. Personal information (such as phone numbers, emails, and chat messages) is encrypted. Phone numbers used for contact syncing are strictly hashed securely using SHA-256 protocols before leaving your device.
