📋 Terms & Conditions

Rental Agreement Terms

📈 Rent Increase Policy

Annual Rent Increase: Rent may increase by 15% yearly based on market conditions and property maintenance costs. Tenants will be notified at least 60 days before any rent increase takes effect.

⏰ Payment Timelines & Status

Due Date: Rent is due on the 1st day of each month. Payment status is automatically updated based on payment dates:

✓ PAID: If rent is paid on or before 1st of the month

⚠ PENDING: If rent is unpaid for 1-2 days after due date

✗ LATE: If rent is unpaid for 3 or more days after due date

Late payments may incur additional charges. Tenants should maintain timely payments to avoid late fees and maintain good standing.

🏠 Property Maintenance

Maintenance Responsibility: Property owner is responsible for major repairs and structural maintenance. Tenants are responsible for keeping the property clean and reporting any issues promptly.

💰 Utility Payments

Electricity & Water: Tenants are responsible for electricity and water consumption charges based on actual usage. Rates are determined by local utility providers and may vary seasonally.

📝 Termination Policy

Notice Period: 30 days written notice is required for lease termination. Security deposit will be returned within 15 days of vacating, subject to property inspection.

This agreement is legally binding and subject to local rental laws.
